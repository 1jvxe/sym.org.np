About Us

Sanatan Yuba Manch Nepal is a social organization that is dedicated to helping the community. We've been busy cleaning up the city, giving food to those who need it, and working on other important projects. Recently, we teamed up with BroadPress Infosys to make the #VisitKoshi2025 and #KeepCityClean campaigns even better. Together, we're not just making the city look nicer but also teaching people to take care of it and the environment.

  • Our Mission
  • To actively involve young people in addressing social issues and making a positive impact in their communities.
  • To operate ambulance services to swiftly transport patients to hospitals, ensuring timely medical care and saving lives.
  • To mobilize volunteers and coordinate efforts with other organizations to maximize the impact of our community service initiatives.
  • To educate and empower young people to resist drug addiction,preventing substance abuse and promoting healthy lifestyles.
  • To contribute to tourism development by fostering clean and beautiful environments, attracting visitors, and promoting sustainable tourism practices.
  • To promote the preservation of art, culture, caste, tradition, religion, literature, and other aspects of cultural heritage, fostering appreciation and understanding across generations.
  • To offer emergency assistance programs that provide immediate support to individuals and families facing crises such as homelessness, food insecurity, or natural disasters, ensuring they receive timely help and support in times of need.
