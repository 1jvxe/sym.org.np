Community Service and Humanitarian Support Program

Date:12 April 2026

"We have had the opportunity to provide services in Biratnagar Metropolitan City and other districts, including food for the hungry, water for the thirsty, city cleanliness programs, cow protection, management of lighting on Saba Road, temple cleaning, support at Janata School, and blood, medicine, and treatment assistance for sick and needy people. Let us all come together so that no one is left behind."

MOU between PRACAS Infosys and Sanatan Yuba Manch Nepal

Date:04 April 2024

PRACAS Infosys, which is working in the field of information and technology, has signed a reform agreement with Sanatan Yuba Manch Nepal on Sunday for two campaigns '#KeepCityClean' and '#VisitKoshi2025'. Both presidents signed the agreement.

Janata School Biratnagar Cleanliness Program

Date:

The Sanatan Yuba Manch has launched a Cleanliness Program at Janata School in Biratnagar. This initiative focuses on keeping the school environment clean through regular clean-up activities, waste management efforts, and educational campaigns. Together, we're striving to create a hygienic and pleasant atmosphere for everyone at Janata School.

Dustbin Installation

Date:

Sanatan Yuba Manch is placing dustbins all around Biratnagar to keep our city clean. We want to make it convenient for everyone to throw away trash properly. With bins in parks, streets, and other spots, we're making a cleaner, nicer city for everyone. Let's work together for a cleaner Biratnagar.
